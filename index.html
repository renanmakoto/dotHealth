Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 22, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to dotHealth. Our app provides a simple and user-friendly way to calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) and Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information that you may provide while using our app. Please note that the app functions entirely offline, without requiring an internet connection or the creation of user accounts.

2. Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or data from users. The app is designed to function without requiring any personal information or the creation of user accounts.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not use your data in any way. The app operates entirely on your device, and all calculations are performed locally.

4. Third-Party Services

No Third-Party Data Sharing

Our app does not integrate with or use any third-party services that could collect or share your data.

5. Security

Data Security

As we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to secure. Your use of the app is entirely anonymous, and all calculations are performed on your device.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Policy Updates

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.
